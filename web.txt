How does the internet work?

The internet is the infrastructure that makes up the Web. 
The internet is the network that lets all computers on the web communicate.
Any computer or hardware that is able to connect to a network can be part of the internet.



What happens when you click on a link?

When you click a link your browser is asking that URL to send its files and data.
Your browser gets that URL and uses the DNS to get the IP of that URL to send the info.
Once you have the IP of the URL your browser can start making requests to display files like HTML, CSS, Javascript, etc.
